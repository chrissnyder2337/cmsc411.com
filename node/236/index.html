The Xbox One and the PlayStation 4 are about equally matched from a hardware perspective. The as of June 2013, the PlayStation 4 has 75,000 preoders while the Xbox One has 45,000. Although the PlayStation 4 was announced in February and the Xbox One was announced in May, that could account for the large differences in preorder sales. It will be very hard to judge which one comes out on top. However the Wii U should not be discounted with its unique first party titles. The Wii U has sold 390,000 units from its launch to April according to Nintendo. Although it is off to a slow start, It's sales should get better as more software is released for it.
