Xeon: Introduction

Xeon is a line of multiprocessing- or multi-socket-capable x86 microprocessors from Intel targeted at the non-consumer workstation, server, and embedded system markets. Xeon focuses on high-performance, high-reliability architecture for performance-critical systems. It includes several advanced features to manage hardware errors and guard against malicious software attacks like a NoX (no-executable) bit.
