CPU Benchmarking

When you overclock your CPU, you need to test whether the clock which you have attained is sustainable. In order to do so, you should run something called a "stress-test" for your CPU. A CPU stress test is a program which heavily utilizes CPU resources. For example, calculating many digits of pi or many prime numbers would utilize CPU resources. If your overclocked system-build runs reasonable operating tempertures after hours of heavy stress, then you can safetly assume that your CPU will not overheat when operating in similair (heavy stress) situations.
